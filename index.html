PARIS
Paris is the capital and largest city of France, often called the "City of Light" for its historical significance and beauty. Located on the River Seine, Paris is renowned for landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, world-class museums, and vibrant culture.
NORWAY
Norway is a Nordic country located on the Scandinavian Peninsula in Northern Europe, known for its dramatic fjords, mountainous terrain, and extensive coastline. The capital is Oslo.
BALI
Bali is an island province of Indonesia, famous for its beautiful beaches, lush landscapes, and vibrant cultural traditions. It is the only Hindu-majority region in Indonesia and attracts millions of tourists with its arts, temples, and hospitality.
SWITZER LAND
Switzerland, officially called the Swiss Confederation, is a landlocked country in Central Europe known for its mountainous terrain, including the Swiss Alps and Jura mountains.
VIETNAM
Vietnam, is a country in Southeast Asia known for its long coastline along the South China Sea and its rich history shaped by Chinese influence and colonial struggles.
